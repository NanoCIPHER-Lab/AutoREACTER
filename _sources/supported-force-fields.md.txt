## Supported Force Fields

All classical force fields integrated into **AutoREACTER** are processed via the [LUNAR](https://github.com/CMMRLab/LUNAR) Package. 

1. **PCFF-IFF** 
2. **PCFF** 
3. **Compass**
4. **CVFF-IFF** 
5. **CVFF** 
6. **DRIEDING** 


**Note: Proper installation and path configuration for LUNAR is required in `AutoREACTER` to utilize these force fields.**
